How to Search

There are two type of searches in this ScienceMakers corpus:

  • Hundreds of "ScienceMakers", i.e., the men and women involved in science that were interviewed in the archive
  • Thousands of stories, i.e., what these men and women spoke about

From the Maker Directory page, you will see a set of ScienceMakers. You can issue a search to the biographies and see a resulting set of people. You can use advanced biography-search capabilities, such as whether to search on just the name.

From the Home page, you can issue a search and see a resulting set of stories. It has its own advanced story-search capability as well.
