Privacy Statement

Our Commitment to Privacy

This notice applies to all information collected or submitted on the DIGITAL ARCHIVE. We collect transactions with the DIGITAL ARCHIVE, including searches, video plays, your volunteered comments, and search filter actions. The transactions are marked with an anonymized tag (a series of numbers and letters) that uniquely identifies you, but which by itself is meaningless. No personally identifiable information is collected with the analytics for this site.

The Information We Collect

Your search and other transaction information is used in research reports only in anonymized form. The data is used by researchers to improve the interface to the DIGITAL ARCHIVE, for auditing, research and analysis in order to maintain, protect and improve our services, for ensuring the technical functioning of our network, and for developing new services. We use non-identifying and aggregate information to better design our website and to perhaps in the future to share with advertisers. For example, we may tell an advertiser that X number of individuals visited a certain area on our website.

Our Commitment to Data Security

To prevent unauthorized access, maintain data accuracy, and ensure the correct use of information, we have put in place appropriate physical, electronic, and managerial procedures to safeguard and secure the information we collect online.
